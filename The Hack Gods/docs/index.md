
### The Hack Gods

**A Solo Deduction Game involving your deduction skills to figure out who is stopping the hack. You are The Hack God after all, the hack must commence.**

A group of Hackers are hired to hack something critical. The Feds won't have this, so they will try to stop the Hackers. interlopers want in on the cash too, so they barge into the operation as well.
