### **Actives:**

Sell Illicit Arms - Select a target operative. If they do not have access to the skill Disorganized Murder, give them 1 charge of Disorganized Murder the following night. This charge of Disorganized Murder is removed if it is not used the night it is given. Visit them.

Emergency Extraction (3 charges) - Select an operative to escort, does not occupy them and makes them immune to everything unless otherwise stated.

Desperate Measures (1 charge) - Turn into an Improvised Hacker.

### **Passives:**

None.
