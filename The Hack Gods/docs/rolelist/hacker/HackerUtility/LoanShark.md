### **Actives:**

Receive Loan - Add one fund to your total funds.

Sell Loan (1 night cooldown) - Select a target operative. Visit and occupy them, gaining a fund. (Same as Midnight Meet).

=======================================================================

Hack Target (Moderate, 1 charge) - Select a white node and hack it, dealing 1 HP. Leaves a log.

Download Intel (1 charge) - Select a green node and attempt to download from it. If there is intel, and you download and remove it first, you will get intel (see tips for list of intel). Leaves a log.

Emergency Extraction (1 charge) - Select an operative to escort, does not occupy them and makes them immune to everything unless otherwise stated.

### **Passives:**

Greedy - You passively gain one fund every new night. Funds are used as charges of actives.
