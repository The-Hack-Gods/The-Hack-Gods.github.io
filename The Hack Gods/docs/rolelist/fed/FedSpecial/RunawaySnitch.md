### **Actives:**

Harden Node (Moderate, +1 Wanted Level) - Select a white node, heal the node by 3 HP. Leaves a log.

Midnight Meet (1 night cooldown) - Select a target operative. Visit and occupy them, revealing your identity.

Experienced Hiding (3 charges) - Move your hideout, protecting yourself unless you are occupied.

Snitch to Cops (2 night cooldown, +1 Wanted Level) - Select a target operative. Attempt to arrest the operative with local cops. Do not visit them.

Disorganized Murder (+1 Wanted Level) - Select an operative and murder them. Visit the target.

### **Passives:**

Same Old Me - Keep your original subclass from the role you turned from.
