- Hackers want to hack the target node, or kill all Feds. They have a time limit to complete their objective and are the major faction.
<br>
- You choose a Hack God in order to control the operation.
<br>
- Each Hack God has a set of actions they can do.
<br>
- Hack Gods can also choose to execute one operative per night. If they execute wrongly enough times, they will be arrested.
<br>
- Hack Gods know everyone's claim and hack health, but not the whole node topology.
<br>
- Hack Gods can also influence everyone's actions. Hackers can be selected to cancel their action, or change their action to another operative, but not change their specific action chosen.
<br>
