- The basic ruleset is as follows:
<br>
- You can choose a faction, and a faction leader.
<br>
- Hackers want to hack the target node, or kill all Feds. They have a time limit to complete their objective and are the major faction.
<br>
- Feds want to arrest/kill all Hackers or prevent the target node from being hacked. They can wait out the time limit and are the minor faction.
<br>
- Interloopers have a specific win condition and to extract. In Hacker or Fed mode, they won't have an objective and simply exist in the operation.
<br>
- As a Hack God you must make sure the Hackers win. As a Fed Leader, you must make sure the Feds win. As an Interlooper, you must complete your objective.
<br>
- The roles, operatives, and node topology are randomly generated. Each scenario is randomly generated, with some set of parameters that can limit the generation (such as rolelist, operative count, and node topology generated.)
<br>
- Each game has a number of nights that is the time limit. There are only night phases.
<br>
- The first phase starts with a claim and claimed action. Actions will be done between each night and the rolelist and consequences will be shown afterwards, with claims and claimed action.
<br>
- Roles have a set of actions they can take, at any night. Roles can have passives.
<br>
- Operatives have a role and faction, randomly generated based on rolelist. They also get a unique identifier (color).
<br>
- Many roles will lie. These roles will lie based on their role to try to mimic their claim. Investigative abilities do go through their lie, but there are abilities that may affect those investigative results.
<br>
- Depending on the faction ruleset, you may have more or less information. Read the specific ruleset per faction.
<br>
- Actions can be done on operatives or nodes.
<br>
- In order to hack a node, each operative must damage the node until it is hacked. Nodes have a health bar that must be hacked.
<br>
- Laptops have 5 HP, and Servers have 10 HP. Very Low deals 1 HP damage, Low deals 2 HP, Moderate deals 3 HP, High deals 4 HP, and Very High deals 5 HP.
<br>
- Download intel and other sources of intel can include:
    <br>
    - Subclass of an operative
    <br>
    - Faction of an operative
    <br>
    - Role in play
    <br>
    - Remaining number of a faction (e.g. number of Hackers remaining)
    <br>
    - An action that was done last night
    <br>
    - A node that has intel
    <br>
    - Who dealt the most damage to a node
    <br>
    - Target node
