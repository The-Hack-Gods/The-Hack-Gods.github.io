- Hackers want to hack the target node, or kill all Feds and other opposing factions. They have a time limit to complete their objective and are the major faction.
<br>
- You choose a Hack God in order to control the operation.
<br>
- Hackers get access to a set of trusted operatives. The Hack God can control those operatives. The rest of the operatives can be any faction and must be deciphered to figure out who is a Hacker and who isn't.
<br>
- Each Hack God has a set of actions they can do.
<br>
- Hack Gods can also choose to execute one operative per night.
<br>
- Hackers do not know the whole node topology. They only know node health.
<br>
- Hackers must figure out each operative's role using their action space.
<br>
