- W3C want to arrest/kill all Hackers or prevent the target node from being hacked. They can wait out the time limit and are the minor faction. W3C is designed to sacrifice their own operatives, like a cult.
<br>
- W3C get access to a set of operatives. These operatives must contain at least one killing operative. The W3C Leader can control those operatives.
<br>
- Interlopers do not know the whole node topology. They only know node health.
<br>
- W3C must figure out each operative's role using their action space.
<br>
- Many actions can increase or decrease wanted level. Your operatives will slowly be more or less wanted. If your wanted level is too high, an operative will be killed. This operative can be chosen. If all operatives die before your objective is finished, you lose.
<br>
- Actives and passives that deceive or compromise will be labelled by how Wanted Level is changed, with Wanted Level total HP as 5 HP.
